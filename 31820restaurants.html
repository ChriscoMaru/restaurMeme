These Charlotte spots will have your mouth water!

Whether you're looking to go all out or just have a nice bite on your lunch break, this list has got you covered. These start!

  1. Lunch Choice:Mr. Tokyo

    2. With is large assortment of Japanese and Japanese-inspired dishes, Mr. Tokyo has got you covered. Don't forget to catch the lunch special for a real feast.

  2. Sandwich Spot:Banh Mi Bros

    3. A simple menu, but a complex assortment of flavors, Banh Mi bros has recently placed on the nations top 50 Restaurants! Be sure to check out the Foh Banh Mi for a taste you won't forget.

  3. For going all out:Sea level

    4. For those who are either looking to entertain their already sophisticated palletes, or for someone looking for a taste of the high life look to Sea Level. This restaurant is known for its oysters and sea food. Ever had a raw oyster? No? Well, what are you waiting for?

  4. Chewy Treats:Honey Buns

    5. Ever like, craved some steamed buns cause you just watched an episode of your favorite anime? Check out The Super G Mart, and inside you will find buns for days, big pimpin.

  5. Wild Card:Optimist Hall

    6. Optimist Hall has so much its hard to describe. Just know that if youre a foodie, you will not have a bad time at the hall!
